syntax
Pattern Description
.       Any character
^       Beginning of line
$       End of line
[]      Character classes*
        [A-Za-z] [0-9] [.]
|       or operator
( )     Group delimiter

command & boundary
\d      Any digit [0-9]
\D      Any non-digit [^0-9]
\w      Word character [A-Za-z_0-9]
\W      Any non-word character
\s      White space
\b      Word boundary

multiplicity
?       0 or 1
*       0 or more
+       1 or more
{n}     Exactly n times
{n,}    At least n times
{n,m}   At least n times but no more than m times
